Situation:

During the COVID-19 lockdown, the University of Southern Denmark (SDU) sought a playful way to introduce children to its different faculties—despite physical restrictions.

Task:

  • Create a digital learning experience tailored to children
  • Present the various SDU faculties in an engaging and age-appropriate way
  • Design intuitive and playful UI and gameplay

Action:

  • Collaborated in a team of 7 part-time developers over 5 months
  • Led the Gameplay and UI design to ensure intuitive interaction and fun progression
  • Developed and tested playful mini-games representing SDU's faculties
  • Launched the game on the App Store during the pandemic (later retired)

Results:

  • Delivered with great enthusiasm from the project owner
  • Praised for its creativity and relevance during remote learning times
  • Helped children engage with university life in a digital format
